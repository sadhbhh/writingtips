WritingTips

Don't Ramble

Rambling, using fluff words and adding unecessary details can make your work boring and less powerful. Try to use language that is concise and interesting.

Punctuation and Grammar

Punctuation is always important. Commas, full stops, semi-colons, etc make it easier for you to control the flow of your thoughts, and can make it much easier for other people to read and understand your work.

Edit Edit Edit!!

You need to be ruthless when you edit. Shorten, delete, and rewrite anything that does not add to the meaning. It’s okay to write in a casual style, but don’t inject extra words without good reason. To make this easier, break your writing into three steps: 1) Write the entire text. 2) Set your text aside for a few hours or days. 3) Return to your text fresh and edit.
